http://choggi.org/misc/CuteButtonsCrystalSVG/css/mosaic.org.png (rename to mosaic.png)

2. place mosaic.png in \Program Files\Mozilla Firefox\ (it can also be anything that uses Stylish or UserChrome.css)

3. open this in firefox, click the stylish icon and choose "Install file" or "Add File to Stylish"
(if you use UserChrome.css remember to remove the @namespace line)
http://choggi.org/misc/CuteButtonsCrystalSVG/css/Icons.Gecko.css

4. open css, find and replace all

file:///C:/program files/mozilla firefox/mosaic.png
(or where ever you want)

5. restart firefox
